Electronics Manufacturing

GRT Electronics is a contract manufacturer offering full production services for our clients in a leaner and cost-effective approach. We are dedicated to on-time, schedule-driven performance with open communication to our clients.

We primarily serve customers searching for the following services:

  • Quick-turn prototype production
  • Contract manufacturing usually 1~1000 pcs runs, but have gone as high as 5000 for a single run
  • Inventory management and partial turnkey services
  • Prototype/Production design assistance
  • Consultation; problem solving; cost reduction; process/manufacturing engineering
  • Component/XRAY services

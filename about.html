Who We Are

GRT Electronics is a leading contract manufacturer specializing in PCB assembly and mechanical control solutions. With decades of combined experience, we've built a reputation for delivering precision, reliability, and innovation.

Our team of skilled professionals is committed to providing exceptional service and high-quality products to clients across diverse industries including automotive, medical, aerospace, and industrial sectors.

Why Choose Us

  • State-of-the-art manufacturing facilities
  • ISO certified quality assurance processes
  • Rapid prototyping and custom solutions
  • Dedicated customer support team
  • Competitive pricing without compromising quality

Our Mission

To deliver innovative and reliable electronics manufacturing solutions that exceed our customers' expectations. We are committed to continuous improvement, technological advancement, and building long-term partnerships with our clients.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred contract manufacturer in the electronics industry, recognized for our quality, innovation, and customer-centric approach. We aim to set industry standards for excellence and sustainability.

Electronics Manufacturing

GRT Electronics is a contract manufacturer offering full production services for our clients electronics and electro-mechanical products. We are dedicated to responsive, schedule-driven performance with open communication to our clients.

We primarily serve customers searching for the following services:

  • Quick-turn prototype production
  • Contract manufacturing supply 1-2000 unit runs, but have gone as high as 5000 for a single run
  • Turn-key consigned, consigned kit builds
  • Prototypes/Production design assistance
  • Consultation problem-solving cost reduction, production engineering
  • Component RoHS services
